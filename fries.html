Dish 1

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Oil for frying
  • salt

Instructions

  1. Wash and peel the potatoes. Cut them into even-sized sticks, about 1/4 to 1/2 inch thick. Rinse the cut potatoes in cold water to remove excess starch.
  2. Soaking the potatoes in cold water for about 30 minutes to 1 hour can help remove more starch, resulting in crispier fries. Pat them dry thoroughly before frying.
  3. Carefully add a handful of potato sticks to the hot oil
